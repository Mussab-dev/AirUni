Welcome to Air University

The mission of Air University is to achieve excellence in teaching and research for producing graduates with sound professional knowledge, integrity of character, a keen sense of social responsibility and a passion for lifelong learning. The University shall stand committed to creating an environment conducive for attracting the best students, faculty, and supporting staff for contributing to the development of a prosperous, peaceful, and enlightened society.

UnderGraduate Programs

Students' professional development and foundation building are both facilitated by their undergraduate education. Our diverse UG programmes turn students into successful people.

Master Programs

Graduate programmes encourage students to be inquisitive and enhance their knowledge and expertise in specialised sectors. As a result, AU has the most MS-level programmes available.

PHD programs

The goal of the PhD programmes at AU is to provide information that fuels economic progress. However, AU sought to produce knowledge for professional development.

Student Portal

The student Portal also offers details about the courses that are offered, transcripts, email services, timetables, and exam schedules.

Leave Portal

An online leave system is used to streamline the process of approving leave requests. Features like Email Notification and Leaving Record are available.

Fee Portal

Fee Portal offers information on fees for enrolled students, and a communications platform will provide a Challan form for payments made to banks.

AU Multan Campus

The AU Multan Campus is dedicated to providing a high-quality education and an environment for personal and academic growth.

AU Kamra Campus

AU Kamra Campus, part of Air University in Pakistan, focuses on engineering and technical education, especially aerospace and avionics.

AU Islamabad

AU Islamabad Campus is the main campus of Air University in Pakistan, offering a wide range of programs in engineering, business, social sciences, and IT

AU Kharian

AU Kharian Campus, an extension of Air University, serves the Gujrat region with programs in engineering, IT, and business.

AFFILIATED INSTITUTES

Bilquis College Nur Khan Base, Rawalpindi

The institution has been a focal point for the operations of the Pakistan Air Force Women Association (PAFWA) as well as for the education and training of women since it was founded in 1984.

College of Education, Peshawar

The Institute is in a unique position to equip teachers for secondary schools as well as Montessori institutes.

Aero Medical Institute, PAF Base, Masroor, Karachi

The largest airfield used by the Pakistan Air Force is PAF Base Masroor. It is located in the Sindh province's Mauripur neighborhood of Karachi.
